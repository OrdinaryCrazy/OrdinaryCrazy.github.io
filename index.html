Bio

Howdy! I am currently a Software Engineer (R \& D) in Tiktok.Inc (from Feb. 2023 to Now). I obtained my master's degree from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University in 2022. and obtained my bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China in 2020.

My major focus on algorithms and machine learning. Specifically, I am familiar with (1) software building and algorithms, and (2) machine learning algorithms for graph data (Graph-Neural-Network).
